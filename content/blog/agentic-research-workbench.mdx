This is a working outline for a research workspace built around paper reading,
structured extraction, figure generation, and project-local automation.

## Purpose

The goal is to keep research artifacts close to the place where they become
useful: code, diagrams, notes, datasets, and reusable prompts should live in a
workflow that can be inspected and revised.

## Current Shape

- Read papers into structured notes.
- Extract methods, claims, limitations, and figure logic.
- Turn dense material into reusable visual and software artifacts.
- Keep each generated result traceable to its source.

## Next

The workbench should make it easier to move from reading to implementation
without losing context. The next step is to standardize the input folder,
article metadata, and output assets so each project can be resumed cleanly.
